Who we are

Agentic Architect is a blog and newsletter about AI coding tools and .NET developer infrastructure, written by the Agentic Architect team. The goal is honest, technically rigorous content for senior C#/.NET developers.

The affiliate relationship

Some posts and pages contain affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no extra cost to you.

Amazon Associates: Hardware pages use direct Amazon.co.uk links with tag agenticstanda-21, pasted manually from SiteStripe. We do not route Amazon through redirects, scrape Amazon content, or use Amazon Program Content in automated tools or AI pipelines. Outbound clicks are counted via GoatCounter only. We do not display live Amazon prices next to buy links.

Program Content: We do not fetch, store, or process Amazon product data, images, or text through our affiliate engine, MCP server, or any LLM. Comparison pages are original editorial content.

Other programs: Non-Amazon affiliate links (where used) may be routed through our own click tracker (e.g. agentic-architect.dev/go/<code>) before redirecting to the vendor. Amazon is excluded from this pattern.

We use affiliate links because they align incentives correctly: we earn only if someone finds the recommendation genuinely useful and acts on it.

Our editorial policy

We do not accept payment for positive coverage. Affiliate relationships do not influence what we recommend, how we benchmark tools, or what weaknesses we report. If a tool we're affiliated with is worse on a given metric, we say so, as in the Cursor vs Rider comparison.

We will always disclose which links are affiliate links using the label "(affiliate link)" or by routing them through our own tracker. This page is linked from any post that contains affiliate links.
