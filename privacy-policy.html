Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: we collect only what we need to run the site, deliver the kit, and (if you opt in) send the newsletter. We do not sell your data. Analytics are privacy-friendly. You can unsubscribe or ask us to delete your data at any time.

Who we are

Agentic Architect ("we", "us") operates agentic-architect.dev, a site and newsletter for senior C#/.NET developers using AI coding tools. The data controller for this site is Agentic Architect. Contact: agenticstandardcontact@gmail.com.

What we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may process the following:

Data When Why
Email address Newsletter signup (MailerLite) or product checkout (Payhip) Deliver the free starter kit, send the welcome sequence, fulfil paid kit orders, and respond to support requests
Name (optional) MailerLite signup form, if you provide it Personalise emails
Payment details Payhip checkout for the £9 kit (Stripe processes payment) Processed by Payhip and Stripe. We do not store card numbers on our servers.

Hashed click data When you follow certain short links (e.g. /go/*) Measure which recommendations are useful. IP addresses are hashed with a daily salt; we do not store raw IPs.

Legal basis (UK GDPR)

We process personal data on these bases:

  • Contract: to deliver digital products you purchase (full kit via Payhip).
  • Consent: for the newsletter, optional analytics, and optional marketing cookies. You can withdraw consent by unsubscribing or changing cookie preferences.
  • Legitimate interests: to operate and secure the site, measure aggregate traffic, and improve content. We balance this against your rights and offer opt-out via the cookie banner.

Third-party services

We use trusted processors to run the funnel. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Payhip — checkout and digital delivery for the Agentic Architect Kit (£9 one-time). Payhip Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing inside Payhip checkout. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • MailerLite — newsletter signup, automations, and email delivery. MailerLite Privacy Policy
  • GoatCounter — privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics (enabled only if you accept analytics cookies). GoatCounter privacy
  • GitHub Pages — static site hosting.

Cookies and similar technologies

Essential storage (cookie consent choice) is required for the site to remember your preferences. Optional cookies include GoatCounter analytics and MailerLite form features. Use the cookie banner (Accept / Reject / Manage) to control optional categories. Rejecting optional cookies does not block access to the site or purchases.

For affiliate relationships and how we label sponsored links, see our Affiliate & Sponsorship Disclosure.

How long we keep data

  • Newsletter: until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete your contact.
  • Purchase records: as long as needed for tax, refunds, and support (typically up to 7 years for financial records where required).
  • Analytics: aggregated counts retained by GoatCounter per their retention policy.
  • Cookie preferences: until you clear browser storage or we bump the consent version key.

Your rights

If you are in the UK or EEA, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email agenticstandardcontact@gmail.com. You may also complain to the ICO (UK) or your local supervisory authority.

Newsletter subscribers can unsubscribe instantly via the link in any email.

Children

This site and products are aimed at professional software developers. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

International transfers

Some processors (Payhip, Stripe, MailerLite, GoatCounter) may process data outside the UK. They use appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses where applicable.
